# .design-context.md: CoffeeDex Rebuy Memory

## Project
- **Name**: CoffeeDex
- **Type**: Korea-first specialty coffee memory, quick private record, Rebuy List, and Rebuy Intelligence product
- **Languages**: Korean (primary), English (secondary)

## Target Audience
- **Role**: Hardcore home brewers, specialty coffee hobbyists, baristas, roasters
- **Tech level**: Moderate to high; uses mobile web at cafes and home brewing bars
- **Age range**: 20-40
- **Context**: Capturing private 20-second coffee memories, scanning bean labels at home or in cafes, reviewing rebuy cues, and exporting owned JSON/CSV data

## Brand Personality
- Artisanal and precise (sensory-driven)
- Warm, welcoming, and privacy-minded
- Sophisticated yet highly accessible
- Elegant, premium, and clean (no bloated corporate layout)

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Warm Premium Canvas (Craft paper, raw ceramic, and coffee shop tones)
- **Mood**: Tactile, cozy, clean, high-precision
- **Surface treatment**: Soft cards with delicate borders, fine shadows, and paper-like container fills
- **Visual accents**: Caramel Gold (#b87d4b) and Golden Honey (#dfa857) for high-lights, Espresso Brown (#2c1d11) for high-contrast primary elements
- **Texture**: Soft dithered noise overlays for flavor wheels, fine paper-like canvas backgrounds

## Typography
- **Body & UI**: Pretendard Variable (CJK optimized for Korean readability) + system-ui fallback
- **Headings (Brand/English)**: Playfair Display / Georgia (serif) for display numbers and English headers
- **Headings (Korean)**: Pretendard Variable Bold for clean, high-impact Korean headings
- **Justification**: CJK alignment and line-heights (1.7 for body, 1.3 for headings)

## Color Direction
- **Canvas Background**: Warm Canvas Cream (#f7f7f4)
- **Primary Text**: Rich Espresso (#2c1d11)
- **Secondary Text**: Roasted Cocoa (#5c4a3c)
- **Primary Accent**: Caramel Gold (#b87d4b)
- **Warning/Error**: Cinnamon Crimson (#d9463e)
- **Success/Entitled**: Leaf Matcha Green (#4d7c54)
- **Borders & Dividers**: Delicate Sand (#e6e1da)
- **Card Surface**: Fresh Ceramic White (#ffffff) with 80% opacity backdrop blur

## Accessibility
- **Level**: WCAG AA minimum
- **Motion**: prefers-reduced-motion support required
- **Contrast**: Minimum 4.5:1 for body and interaction elements
- **Touch targets**: 44x44pt minimum on mobile

## Reference Sites
- [bluebottlecoffee.com](https://bluebottlecoffee.com): clean craft branding, warm paper tones, beautiful spacing
- [leica-camera.com](https://leica-camera.com): precise layout, premium typography, spacious editorial design
- [monocle.com](https://monocle.com): editorial cream backgrounds, rich serif/sans pairing, premium paper feel

## Component Preferences
- **Base**: shadcn/ui for all base elements (Cards, Buttons, Inputs, Dialogs)
- **Animations**: motion/react for memory cards, scan loading, and slider transitions
- **Effects**: canvas-confetti for Stripe checkout celebration, custom SVG drawing for tasting wheels
