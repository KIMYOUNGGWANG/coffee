# Wave 2: Unit Economics and Passport Threshold

## Findings

- Estimated marginal scan-plus-first-month-card cost is roughly KRW 1.12 / 5.20 / 34.84 in low/base/high scenarios. Gemini extraction is not the main normal-use risk; abuse and repeated image delivery are.
- KRW 14,900 lifetime can support ordinary enthusiast usage, but contractual unlimited AI creates unbounded tail risk. Lifetime archive ownership plus fair-use scan allowance and throttling is safer.
- Five logs can reveal an explicitly labeled early snapshot. A normal passport should wait for roughly 10+ logs plus coverage checks; 20 logs still must not be called definitive taste DNA.
- Small samples manufacture false precision. Show literal counts, missing coverage and aligned dot plots rather than a complete filled radar polygon.
