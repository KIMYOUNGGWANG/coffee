# Wave 2: Closest Competitor and Review Corpus

## Findings

- BeanBook already covers bag scan, structured bean data, visual shelf/card/flavor views, yearly insights, AI Q&A, recipes, discovery, drops and deals. CoffeeDex cannot position AI scan + shelf + insights as a novel bundle.
- BeanBook's breadth suggests a viable counter-position: quiet, private, cross-platform, low-administration coffee memory focused on what I drank, liked and would repurchase.
- Direct review evidence across competitors repeatedly exposes sync, search, entry-reset, export and reliability issues. The corpus is sparse and not frequency-valid, so these are design risks rather than ranked market demand.
- Existing products make photo, search, export and sync increasingly table stakes. CoffeeDex's stronger promise must be the whole fast-and-trustworthy loop, not any one checkbox.
