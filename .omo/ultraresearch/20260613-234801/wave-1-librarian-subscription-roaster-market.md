Worker: `019ec4e5-3d3a-7772-85e8-9b7fd2c10f79`

## Digest

- CoffeeDex should partner with discovery/convenience lanes and avoid head-on competition with premium bean clubs that already own brand, fulfillment, or hardware moats.
- Trade Coffee is a strong partner model: quiz, roaster marketplace, many coffees, gifts, referral/corporate gifting, and roaster lead-gen.
- Atlas Coffee Club owns the "world coffee journey/gift" story; partnership or inspiration only.
- Cometeer owns premium convenience via capsule format and app profile; not the right direct competitor.
- Fellow Drops is a weekly offer/audience capture loop tied to hardware commerce.
- Blue Bottle is a vertically integrated premium cafe/subscription brand; CoffeeDex should not compete directly.
- Best CoffeeDex lane: community + lead-gen layer for local roasters with optional subscription/affiliate routing, not bean fulfillment.
