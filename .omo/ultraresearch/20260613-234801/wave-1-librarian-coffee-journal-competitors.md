# Wave 1 Librarian: Consumer Coffee Journal Competitors

Worker: `019ec4e5-34c0-7242-8303-85246cbc7ce1`

## Digest

- Beanconqueror: direct high-depth competitor; open-source and free; strong brew logs, bean inventory, roast tracking, water section, SCA cupping, smart device support, and local/offline privacy.
- Filtru: guided brewing and roaster commerce competitor; recipe guidance, scale support, bean journal, and roaster/business layer.
- Aeromatic: narrow AeroPress recipe/timer app; strong niche utility, not broad journaling.
- Acaia apps: hardware-centric free apps that monetize via scales/grinders, not software.
- CoffeeDex differentiation should avoid head-on depth against Beanconqueror and instead own cloud memory, shareable cards, AI scan/story, social/export flows, and consumer-friendly recall.
