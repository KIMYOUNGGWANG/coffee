## Key Expansion Decision

The naming problem is not just "CoffeeDex sounds awkward." The deeper issue is that "Dex" implies database/catalog/completionist mechanics, while the product thesis is about personal taste memory, meaningful recap, and paid digital artifacts. A better brand should feel like a taste archive rather than a catalog clone.
