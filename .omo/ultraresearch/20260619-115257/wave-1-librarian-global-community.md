## Key Findings

- Repeated pain: users want logging, repeatability, and memory without spreadsheet-like complexity.
- Coffee app abandonment drivers: too many taps, typing during brewing, overwhelming UI, unreliable device connectivity, and no obvious next-cup improvement.
- Beginners want guided structure; advanced users value customizable parameters and device integration.
- Discovery, community, subscription, and cafe passporting are adjacent categories, not substitutes for a fast memory/logging core.
